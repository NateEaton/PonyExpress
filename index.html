About This Archive

This site preserves all issues of The Pony Express from the 1974–1975 school year. Each issue has been scanned and processed with OCR to make the text searchable within the PDF.

Click any cover below to open the full issue in a new tab.

How to Use

  • Use the left/right scroll to browse covers.
  • Click a cover to open the full PDF (will open in a new tab).
  • Use Ctrl+F (or Cmd+F on Mac) inside the PDF to search by name or topic.
